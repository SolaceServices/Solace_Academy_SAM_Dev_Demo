**Version 7.1 | Effective Date: February 1, 2025**
*Applies to all Acme Retail channels: In-Store, acmeretail.com, and Acme Mobile App*

## Overview

Acme Retail is committed to customer satisfaction. We want every purchase to meet your expectations, and we've designed our return and refund process to be as simple and flexible as possible. This document outlines our standard policies, exceptions, and procedures for store associates, customer service representatives, and fulfillment center staff.

## 1. Standard Return Window

| Purchase Channel | Standard Return Window |
|---|---|
| In-Store | 60 days from date of purchase |
| acmeretail.com | 60 days from delivery date |
| Acme Mobile App | 60 days from delivery date |
| Marketplace (third-party) | Subject to marketplace seller's policy |

The return window begins on the date of purchase (in-store) or the confirmed delivery date (online/app orders), as recorded in Acme's order management system.

---

## 2. Refund Methods

Refunds are issued to the original payment method wherever possible.

| Original Payment Method | Refund Method | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Credit or Debit Card | Back to original card | Allow 3–7 business days to appear |
| Acme Gift Card | New Acme Gift Card | Issued immediately at return |
| Cash | Cash (in-store) or mailed check | Checks issued for amounts over $200 |
| Acme Store Credit | Store Credit | Credited to AcmeRewards account |
| Buy Now, Pay Later (Affirm) | Back to Affirm account | Affirm manages customer-facing refund |
| PayPal / Digital Wallet | Back to originating wallet | 3–5 business days |
| Apple Pay / Google Pay | Back to linked card | 3–7 business days |

**Note for Associates:** Never issue a cash refund for a purchase that was not made with cash. Always match refund method to original tender. Exceptions require manager approval.

## 3. Condition Requirements

Items must be returned in the following condition to qualify for a full refund:

- **Unused and unaltered:** Items must not have been worn, washed, assembled, or modified.
- **Original packaging:** Preferred, but not always required (see category exceptions below).
- **All components included:** Accessories, manuals, cables, and any bundled items must be present.
- **Tags attached:** Apparel and footwear must have original tags attached.

Items returned in a condition that does not meet these standards may be subject to a restocking fee of up to 15%, or may only qualify for store credit at current selling price. Associates have discretion to accept returns that fall slightly outside these guidelines with manager approval.

## 4. Category-Specific Policies

### 4.1 Apparel & Footwear
- 60-day return window with tags attached and unworn.
- Items laundered or altered are not eligible for return.
- Swimwear and intimate apparel must have hygiene liner intact.

### 4.2 Electronics & Technology
- 30-day return window (shorter than standard due to manufacturer constraints).
- Must be returned with all original packaging, accessories, and documentation.
- Devices must be factory reset and deactivated (e.g., Find My iPhone disabled) prior to return. Associates should verify this at the point of return.
- Open-box electronics may be subject to a 10% restocking fee.
- Software, downloadable content, and activated SIM cards are non-returnable.

### 4.3 Furniture & Large Items
- 30-day return window.
- Must be unassembled or in the same condition as delivered.
- Customer is responsible for returning the item to a store or scheduling a pickup (fee of $49.99 applies for scheduled return pickups).
- Items showing signs of assembly, damage, or excessive wear will not be accepted.

### 4.4 Mattresses & Bedding
- Mattresses: 100-night sleep trial. Pickup and full refund arranged if unsatisfied after a minimum 30-night break-in period.
- Pillows, mattress toppers, and bedding protectors: Returnable within 60 days, sealed and unused only.
- Opened bedding (sheets, duvets, pillow covers): Returnable within 60 days only if laundered and undamaged.

### 4.5 Grocery & Perishables
- Perishable items (food, fresh flowers, specialty items from the Acme Market section) are non-returnable unless defective or incorrectly described.
- Non-perishable grocery items may be returned within 30 days, unopened and undamaged.

### 4.6 Beauty & Personal Care
- Returnable within 60 days if unopened and in original condition.
- Opened items may be returned within 14 days if the customer is unsatisfied, at the associate's discretion using the "Customer Satisfaction Return" override code (CSR-01).
- Prescription or medicated products are non-returnable.

### 4.7 Toys & Baby Gear
- Standard 60-day return window.
- Car seats cannot be returned if they have been in a vehicle involved in an accident. Associate should ask the customer directly.
- Recalled items: Follow the Recall Procedure in the Associate Playbook (Section 12).

### 4.8 Seasonal & Holiday Items
- Seasonal items (holiday décor, seasonal apparel, garden) must be returned before or on the last day of the season/holiday, or within 30 days of purchase — whichever comes first.
- Exception: the Holiday Return Extension (see Section 11).

### 4.9 Jewelry & Watches
- 60-day return window.
- Fine jewelry (diamonds, gold, sterling silver) must be returned with original certificate of authenticity.
- Items showing signs of wear or alteration (resizing, engraving) are non-returnable.
- Watches must include original box, crown, and all included accessories.

## 5. Exceptions & Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned under any circumstances:

- Gift cards and store credit (except as required by law)
- Downloadable software, digital content, and activation codes
- Prescription medications and customized medical equipment
- Items marked "Final Sale" or "As-Is" at point of purchase
- Hazardous materials (propane tanks, certain chemicals)
- Opened consumable health products (vitamins, supplements)
- Items purchased from a third-party Marketplace seller (subject to that seller's policy)
- Items reported as lost or stolen

---

## 6. Return Without a Receipt

Acme Retail can look up purchases in the following ways when a receipt is unavailable:

1. **AcmeRewards Account:** Purchase history is stored for up to 5 years.
2. **Credit/Debit Card Lookup:** Associate can search by card number (last 4 digits) and approximate date.
3. **Order Number:** For online/app purchases, order confirmation email or app order history.
4. **Barcode Scan:** In some cases, a product scan can verify current price and recent purchase records.

**If no purchase record can be located:**
- Associates may issue store credit for the current selling price of the item, not to exceed $75 per transaction per customer per day.
- A valid government-issued photo ID is required and will be logged.
- More than 3 no-receipt returns per calendar year on a single ID will trigger a return review flag in the system.

## 7. Loyalty Program Returns

**AcmeRewards Members:**
- Points earned on the original purchase will be deducted from the member's account upon return.
- If the member has already redeemed points from the earned balance, the refund will be reduced accordingly.
- If a reward coupon was used at checkout, the coupon value will be refunded as store credit, not cash.

**AcmeRewards Elite & Elite+ Members:**
- Extended return window of 90 days (instead of the standard 60).
- Electronics and furniture extended to 45 days (instead of 30).
- No-receipt returns up to $150 per transaction.

## 8. Online & App Order Returns

### 8.1 Return by Mail

1. Log in to acmeretail.com or the Acme app.
2. Navigate to **Orders > Return Item**.
3. Select the item(s), choose the return reason, and print the prepaid return shipping label.
4. Drop off at any UPS location or schedule a pickup.
5. Prepaid return shipping is free for orders over $35. Orders under $35 incur a $6.99 return shipping fee deducted from the refund.

### 8.2 Return In-Store (Buy Online, Return In-Store — BORIS)

Customers may return online orders to any Acme Retail store location. They should bring:
- The order confirmation email or app order number
- The item(s) in original condition
- A valid photo ID (for orders over $100)

Associates process BORIS returns using the **BORIS workflow** in the POS system. Select "Online Return" and enter the order number. The system will validate eligibility and generate the refund.

### 8.3 Curbside Return Drop-Off

Available at select locations. Customer initiates via the app, receives a check-in code, and an associate retrieves the return from the vehicle. Curbside returns are processed within 1 business day. Refunds are issued to the original payment method.

## 9. In-Store Return Procedures (Associate Reference)

**Step-by-step process:**

1. Greet the customer and ask for the receipt or order number.
2. Verify the item against the return policy (channel, category, condition, window).
3. Open the POS return screen. Scan the item barcode.
4. Select the appropriate return reason code (see table below).
5. Issue the refund to the original tender.
6. If override is required (outside window, no receipt, damaged), enter your Manager Override Code and document the reason.
7. Print and hand the customer a return confirmation receipt.
8. Place the returned item in the appropriate bin: Resaleable, Defective, or Destroy.

**Return Reason Codes:**

| Code | Reason |
|---|---|
| RR-01 | Changed mind / no longer needed |
| RR-02 | Item defective or damaged |
| RR-03 | Wrong item received |
| RR-04 | Item not as described |
| RR-05 | Duplicate purchase |
| RR-06 | Gift return |
| RR-07 | Size/fit issue |
| RR-08 | Late delivery (online orders) |
| RR-09 | Other (requires manager note) |

## 11. Holiday Return Extension

For items purchased between **November 1 and December 31**, the standard return window is extended:

- **Standard items:** Returns accepted through **January 31** of the following year.
- **Electronics & Furniture:** Returns accepted through **January 15** of the following year.
- **Gift receipts:** Items may be returned or exchanged at current selling price through January 31.

The Holiday Return Extension does not apply to Final Sale items, perishables, or non-returnable categories.

## 12. Damaged, Defective, or Incorrect Items

Customers who receive a damaged, defective, or incorrect item are entitled to:

- A full refund, OR
- An exact replacement (subject to availability), OR
- A store credit for the full purchase price plus 10% as a goodwill gesture

For online orders: Customers should report the issue within **14 days of delivery** via the app or website. A prepaid return label will be provided at no cost.

For in-store purchases: The item may be returned directly to any store with no restocking fee, regardless of whether it meets standard condition requirements.

Associates should document defective returns using reason code **RR-02** and flag the item for the vendor return or destruction queue per the product category guidelines.

## 13. Associate Reference: Common Scenarios

**Scenario A: Customer returns electronics 35 days after purchase without box.**
→ Electronics window is 30 days. Return is outside the standard window. Offer store credit at current selling price with manager override (requires Manager Override Code). Document with RR-01 or RR-09.

**Scenario B: Customer returns apparel, tags attached, purchased 55 days ago — has AcmeRewards Elite membership.**
→ Elite members have a 90-day return window. Process as a standard return. Full refund to original tender.

**Scenario C: Customer received wrong item via online order.**
→ Use BORIS workflow, RR-03. Issue full refund or arrange replacement. Provide prepaid return label if item needs to be shipped back. No deduction for return shipping.

**Scenario D: Customer wants to return a Final Sale item.**
→ Final Sale items are non-returnable. Politely explain policy. If customer disputes, escalate to floor manager. Do not override without manager authorization.

**Scenario E: No receipt, no rewards account, paying with card not on file.**
→ Search by card last 4 digits and date range. If not found, issue store credit up to $75 after logging government-issued ID. Flag in system if this is their 3rd+ no-receipt return this year.

## 14. Escalation Procedures

If a customer disputes a return decision:

1. Listen to the customer's concern fully before responding.
2. Re-explain the relevant policy calmly and clearly.
3. Offer an alternative where available (store credit, exchange, repair referral).
4. If the customer remains dissatisfied, call your floor manager or department lead.
5. Managers have the authority to make exceptions up to $250 per transaction.
6. For escalations above $250, contact the **Customer Relations team** at **x3300** or **customercare@acmeretail.com**.
7. Document all exception transactions in the POS system with the manager's override code and a brief note.
