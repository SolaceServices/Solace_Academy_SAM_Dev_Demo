## 2. Our Values

Acme Retail operates according to five core values. These aren't slogans — they shape how we hire, promote, and make decisions.

**1. Customer First**
Every decision starts with the customer experience. We ask: does this make things better for the people we serve?

**2. Integrity Always**
We do what we say we'll do. We're honest with customers, with each other, and with ourselves.

**3. One Team**
We succeed together. Collaboration across departments, channels, and geographies is not optional — it's how we operate.

**4. Continuous Improvement**
We're never done getting better. We encourage every employee to surface ideas and challenge the status quo constructively.

**5. Community Commitment**
We invest in the communities where our stores and fulfillment centers operate, through local hiring, charitable giving, and environmental responsibility.

## 3. Employment Policies

### 3.1 Equal Opportunity Employment

Acme Retail is an equal opportunity employer. We do not discriminate on the basis of race, color, religion, sex, national origin, age, disability, genetic information, sexual orientation, gender identity, veteran status, or any other characteristic protected by law.

### 3.2 Employment Classifications

| Classification | Description |
|---|---|
| **Full-Time** | 32+ hours per week; eligible for full benefits |
| **Part-Time** | Less than 32 hours per week; eligible for select benefits |
| **Seasonal** | Fixed-term employment tied to peak periods (e.g., Holiday, Back-to-School) |
| **Contractor** | Third-party engagement; not eligible for employee benefits |

### 3.3 Background Checks

All offers of employment are contingent on the successful completion of a background check. Roles with financial or data access responsibilities may require additional screening.

### 3.4 Probationary Period

New employees complete a 90-day introductory period. During this time, performance will be assessed by your direct manager. Completion of the probationary period does not alter the at-will nature of employment.

### 3.5 At-Will Employment

Employment at Acme Retail is at-will in all U.S. states, unless a written agreement states otherwise. Either party may end the employment relationship at any time, for any lawful reason, with or without notice.

## 4. Compensation & Benefits

### 4.1 Pay Schedule

All employees are paid bi-weekly (every other Friday). Direct deposit is required and must be set up within your first two weeks via the AcmePay portal at **pay.acmeretail.internal**.

### 4.2 Pay Grades

Acme Retail maintains a structured pay grade system reviewed annually each March:

| Grade | Role Examples | Typical Range (Annual/Hourly) |
|---|---|---|
| G1 | Store Associate, Warehouse Picker | $16–$21/hr |
| G2 | Team Lead, Customer Service Specialist | $20–$27/hr |
| G3 | Department Manager, Fulfillment Supervisor | $52,000–$70,000 |
| G4 | Store Manager, Senior Analyst | $70,000–$95,000 |
| G5 | Regional Director, Senior Manager | $95,000–$140,000 |
| G6+ | VP and above | Individually negotiated |

### 4.3 Benefits Overview

Full-time employees are eligible for the following benefits, effective the first of the month following 30 days of employment:

- **Medical Insurance:** Choice of three plans (Base PPO, Premium PPO, HDHP with HSA). Acme covers 80% of employee premiums.
- **Dental & Vision:** Employer-sponsored; family coverage available.
- **401(k):** Acme matches 100% of the first 4% of eligible compensation contributed.
- **Employee Discount:** 20% off regular-priced merchandise in-store and online.
- **Paid Time Off (PTO):** Accrual begins on day one (see 5.2 for schedule).
- **Parental Leave:** 8 weeks fully paid for primary caregivers; 4 weeks for secondary caregivers.
- **Employee Assistance Program (EAP):** Confidential counseling, financial coaching, and legal services.
- **Tuition Reimbursement:** Up to $5,250/year for approved coursework relevant to your role or career path.

## 5. Work Schedules & Attendance

### 5.1 Scheduling

Store and fulfillment center schedules are published no fewer than 10 days in advance via the **AcmeSchedule** app. Corporate employees work standard business hours (8:00 AM – 5:00 PM local time, Monday–Friday) unless otherwise agreed with their manager.

### 5.2 PTO Accrual

| Years of Service | PTO Accrual Rate |
|---|---|
| 0–1 year | 1 hour per 30 hours worked |
| 1–3 years | 1 hour per 25 hours worked |
| 3–7 years | 1 hour per 20 hours worked |
| 7+ years | 1 hour per 16 hours worked |

PTO must be requested via AcmeSchedule at least 72 hours in advance for planned absences. Emergency absences must be reported to your manager at least 1 hour before your shift starts.

### 5.3 Attendance Standards

More than 3 unexcused absences in a rolling 90-day period will trigger a formal attendance review. Patterns of tardiness (arriving more than 7 minutes after scheduled start) are tracked and addressed through the progressive discipline process.

### 5.4 Overtime

Non-exempt employees who work more than 40 hours in a workweek will receive overtime pay at 1.5× their regular hourly rate. Overtime must be pre-approved by a manager. Unauthorized overtime may result in disciplinary action.

## 6. Code of Conduct

### 6.1 Workplace Behavior

All employees are expected to treat colleagues, customers, and vendors with respect. Harassment, bullying, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind will not be tolerated and may result in immediate termination.

### 6.2 Conflicts of Interest

Employees may not engage in outside employment or business activities that conflict with Acme Retail's interests. This includes working for or owning a stake in a direct competitor. Disclose potential conflicts to your People & Culture Business Partner in writing.

### 6.3 Gifts & Entertainment

Employees may not accept gifts valued at more than $50 from vendors, suppliers, or business partners without written approval from their manager and the Legal team. Cash gifts of any amount are prohibited.

### 6.4 Social Media

When posting on personal social media, employees must not share confidential company information, disclose unreleased product or pricing information, or represent personal views as those of Acme Retail. The use of Acme Retail's name, logo, or trademarks in personal posts requires prior approval from Corporate Communications.

### 6.5 Confidentiality

All employees sign a confidentiality agreement upon hire. This covers customer data, proprietary systems, business strategies, and financial information. Confidentiality obligations survive separation from the company.

## 7. Technology & Data Policy

### 7.1 Acceptable Use

Company-issued devices and systems (laptops, POS terminals, handheld scanners, etc.) are to be used for business purposes. Incidental personal use is permitted but must not interfere with productivity or violate any security policy.

### 7.2 Data Privacy

Acme Retail collects and processes customer and employee personal data in accordance with applicable privacy laws, including CCPA and PIPEDA (for Canadian employees). Employees who handle personal data must complete annual privacy training. Unauthorized access to, sharing of, or misuse of personal data is grounds for immediate termination.

### 7.3 Password & Access Security

- Passwords must be at least 12 characters and changed every 90 days.
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is mandatory for all corporate systems.
- Do not share login credentials under any circumstances.
- Report suspected security incidents immediately to the IT Help Desk at **helpdesk@acmeretail.com** or extension **x4357**.

### 7.4 AI Tools

Acme Retail is actively expanding its use of AI-powered tools across the business. Employees authorized to use AI tools must not input confidential customer data, financial data, or proprietary business strategies into any unsanctioned AI platform. Approved AI tools are listed in the IT Service Catalog.

## 8. Health & Safety

### 8.1 Commitment

Acme Retail is committed to providing a safe working environment. All employees share responsibility for maintaining safe conditions and reporting hazards immediately.

### 8.2 Incident Reporting

Any workplace injury, illness, or near-miss must be reported to your manager within 4 hours of the incident. Formal documentation must be submitted through the **SafetyFirst** portal within 24 hours. Failure to report may affect workers' compensation eligibility.

### 8.3 Emergency Procedures

Each location maintains an Emergency Action Plan posted near all exits. Employees should familiarize themselves with evacuation routes, fire extinguisher locations, and emergency contacts during onboarding.

### 8.4 Substance Policy

Acme Retail maintains a drug- and alcohol-free workplace. Employees are subject to testing for reasonable cause and following any workplace accident. Testing positive will result in termination.

## 9. Training & Development

All new employees complete the Acme Retail Onboarding Program, which includes:

- **Week 1:** Role-specific safety and compliance training
- **Days 30/60/90:** Check-ins with manager and People & Culture partner

All employees must complete the following each calendar year:

| Course | Deadline |
|---|---|
| Code of Conduct Refresher | March 31 |
| Data Privacy & Security | March 31 |
| Workplace Harassment Prevention | June 30 |
| Safety & Emergency Procedures | September 30 |

### 9.3 Career Development

Employees are encouraged to discuss career goals with their manager during quarterly check-ins. Internal job postings are accessible exclusively to current employees for 5 business days before external posting.

## 10. Disciplinary Process

Acme Retail uses a progressive discipline model for most policy violations:

1. **Verbal Warning** — documented in AcmeHR
2. **Written Warning** — signed by employee and manager
3. **Final Written Warning / Performance Improvement Plan (PIP)**
4. **Termination**

Certain violations (theft, harassment, violence, data breaches, gross insubordination) may result in immediate termination without prior steps.

## 11. Separation from Employment

### 11.1 Voluntary Resignation

Please provide at least 2 weeks' written notice to your manager. Managers and above are asked to provide 4 weeks' notice where possible. Final paychecks will include all earned wages and accrued, unused PTO (where required by law).

### 11.2 Return of Property

All company property (devices, access badges, uniforms, keys) must be returned on or before your last day. Unreturned items may be deducted from your final paycheck to the extent permitted by law.

### 11.3 COBRA & Benefits Continuation

Eligible employees will receive COBRA continuation notices within 14 days of separation. Benefits end on the last day of the month in which separation occurs.
